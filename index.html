Frequently Asked Questions

How does care at Beulah Women’s Health work?

Our process is simple and designed to make care accessible: Choose your plan. Select a membership subscription that fits your needs. Not ready for a membership? You can start with a PCOS evaluation or an initial birth control consultation (starting at $75). Schedule your appointment. Click the “Book Now” button to schedule a visit with one of our providers. You’ll receive an email confirmation along with a link to register in the patient portal and complete your intake form. Start your health journey. Begin your journey with care that is both faith-based and evidence-based, supported by personalized treatment and ongoing wellness guidance.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes. At this time, we provide home visits for contraceptive implant procedures only.

What states do you currently serve?

We currently serve Pennsylvania (PA) and Delaware (DE), with additional states coming soon — stay tuned.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes. We accept Cigna, Highmark BlueShield, and Medicare. We also accept all major credit/debit cards, FSA/HSA, and PayPal.

How should I prepare for my initial evaluation or consultation?

Complete all intake forms ahead of your appointment. Weigh yourself on a home scale (without shoes or heavy clothing). Take a home blood pressure (BP) reading. Upload or have your photo ID ready at the time of your visit. Join your appointment using any camera-enabled phone, tablet, or computer.
